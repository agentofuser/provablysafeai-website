# Provably Safe AI

*provablysafe.ai* is a collaborative landing page for the field and community of Provably Safe AI / Safeguarded AI.

## What is Provably Safe AI?

Currently, the go-to introduction to the research field is [Safeguarded AI: constructing safety by design](https://www.aria.org.uk/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/ARIA-Safeguarded-AI-Programme-Thesis-V1.pdf) by David "davidad" Dalrymple.
